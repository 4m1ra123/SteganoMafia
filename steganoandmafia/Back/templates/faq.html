FAQ

These are the most frequently asked questions. If you have any questions, please contact us.

Q: How do I hide a message in an image?

To hide a message, use the “Hide a message” feature and upload your image along with the secret text.

Q: How do I extract a message from an image?

Upload the image with the hidden message and click on “Extract message” to retrieve the text.

Q: What is secure sharing, and how does it work?

Secure sharing generates temporary, encrypted links for sharing sensitive information.

Q: How long are temporary links valid?

Temporary links are valid for up to 24 hours, after which they expire automatically.

Q: Are my data and messages secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted and securely transmitted to ensure your privacy.

Q: What types of files can I use?

You can use common image formats like PNG and JPEG to hide or extract messages.

Q: I forgot my encryption key. What should I do?

Unfortunately, without the encryption key, it's not possible to retrieve the hidden message.
